Biblical & Abrahamic Studies

Project Definition

This project examines the relationship between revelation, transmission, interpretation, and the human understanding of divine reality.

The work approaches Biblical traditions through textual–historical analysis, structural continuity, and observable tensions within the texts.

Rather than beginning from inherited assumptions, the project seeks to follow the portrayal of God within the text itself.

“My pen is the witness and you are the judges.”
