Research Orientation

Revelation Across Sacred History

This section examines the continuity of revelation across prophetic history, tracing how sacred scripture presents divine guidance, covenant transmission, prophetic succession, and future expectation.

The project studies the relationship between revelation, law, prophecy, and theological development from the earliest prophetic traditions to later scriptural interpretations.
